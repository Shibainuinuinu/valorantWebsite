WHAT IS VALORANT

A 5V5 Tactical Shooting Game

Come fight in this 5V5 tactical shooting game. Most commonly played in unrated mode, players must either attack or defend, utilzing abilities and a variety of weapons. Attackers must attempt to defuse the spike and defenders must attempt to defend the spike from being defused. The first to 13 reigns victorous.

Agent

Pick Your Agent

Ranging from duelists to sentinals, choose your agent to go into battle with. Agents are seperated into duelists, initiators, sentinals, and controllers.

Maps

Where Will You Fight Your Battle

Valorant offers a variety of maps to fight on. These maps include Bind, Haven, Ascent, Split, Icebox, Breeze, Fracture, and Pearl.

Arsenal

What Weapon Will You Use To Annihilate Your Enemy

Valorant has a variety of weapons which range from sidearms to heavies. Each gun has a unique playstyle and must be played in a certain way to effectively work.
